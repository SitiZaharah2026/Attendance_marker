Innovation Highlights / Sorotan Inovasi

Three breakthrough features that distinguish CelikSense AI from existing solutions.

🤟

World-first BIM AI Avatar

Personal sign language avatar using Malaysian Sign Language (Bahasa Isyarat Malaysia), personalised to the learner's own face and expression mapping. No other platform offers a locally personalised BIM avatar.

World-First
♾️

5-Disability Unified Platform

Single platform covering visual impairment, hearing impairment, dyslexia, ADHD, and low vision — all with dedicated AI agents. No other educational platform in Malaysia covers all five disability profiles under one roof.

Industry-First in Malaysia
🧠

Cross-Agent AI Personalisation

Learning profile tracks behaviour across all 8 agents and adapts recommendations in real time. The AI synthesises reading speed, focus patterns, comprehension scores, and session history into a unified learner model.

Adaptive Intelligence
